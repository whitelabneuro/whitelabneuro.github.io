Pipelines & Protocols

Reproducible workflows and version-controlled methods

White Lab Neuro is committed to transparent and reproducible research. Our GitHub provides a central home for selected computational pipelines, analysis workflows and version-controlled protocols developed in the lab.

As the lab grows, the WhiteLabNeuro GitHub repository will act as a record of how analyses were run and how methods evolved across projects. This includes both computational workflows and selected experimental protocols linked to specific datasets, projects or manuscripts where appropriate.

Our aim is to support consistency, traceability and open science across the lab’s work, while making it easier for collaborators, trainees and the wider research community to understand and reuse selected resources.

Visit the WhiteLabNeuro GitHub

What you will find

Analysis pipelines

Selected computational workflows used in the lab, including pipelines for transcriptomic and related data analysis.

Version-controlled protocols

Protocols that can be updated over time, helping track how methods develop as projects mature and new approaches are introduced.

Project-linked resources

Materials associated with specific datasets, figures, manuscripts or collaborative projects where public sharing is appropriate.

Documentation and guidance

Supporting notes, readme files and practical information to make workflows easier to interpret, reproduce and adapt.

Why this matters

Research in White Lab brings together human stem-cell models, molecular neuroscience, transcriptomics and data analysis. As these projects become more complex, clear versioning and well-documented workflows become increasingly important.

Keeping pipelines and protocols in a visible, version-controlled space helps support rigorous research practice within the lab and makes it easier to connect methods to publications and datasets over time.

  • Supports reproducibility
  • Tracks method development over time
  • Links workflows to projects and publications
  • Improves internal consistency across the lab
  • Helps collaborators and trainees navigate methods
  • Contributes to open and transparent science

Growing with the lab

This part of the website will expand over time as White Lab develops new workflows, generates new datasets and builds a stronger collection of reusable resources. In the early stages, it will primarily point visitors to the lab’s GitHub as the best place to find pipelines, protocols and related documentation.
